HIPAA Compliance & Security

All templates are designed to help maintain HIPAA compliance when handling protected health information. Remember to:

  • Store completed forms in a secure, locked location
  • Limit access to authorized personnel only
  • Use encryption for digital storage and transmission
  • Follow proper disposal procedures for physical documents
